How do you approach large-scale Azure landing zones?▼
CAF-aligned, end-to-end Infrastructure-as-Code across the full management-group hierarchy (platform, dev, pre-prod, prod), with hardened defaults from day one — TLS 1.2 minimum, no public blob access, least-privilege RBAC. LandingZoneEmpire extends the same governance model into pre-production.
